8. How long we keep your data

We only retain your personal information for as long as we need it for legitimate business purposes (as set out in Section 5) and within the limits of applicable law. If you decide to stop using our services, you may delete your account and your profile will no longer be visible to other users. Once your account is closed, we will delete your personal information as outlined below:

1. To ensure the protection and safety of our users, we retain data for three months after an account is deleted or for one year after it is blocked. During this period, we retain your information in case we need to investigate illegal or malicious activity. The retention of information during this period is based on our legitimate interests as well as the interests of possible third party victims.

2. At the end of the retention period, for security purposes, we will delete your information and retain only limited information for the purposes set out below:

  1. a) We retain certain data in accordance with legal data retention obligations: for example, we retain transaction data for 10 years in accordance with tax and accounting requirements, bank card information during the entire period during which the user can dispute the transaction, and "traffic data" / visit logs for one year to comply with legal data retention obligations. We also retain records of consents provided by users for 5 years to confirm our compliance with applicable law.
  2. b) We retain limited information based on our legitimate interests: customer service records and ancillary data and inaccurate download/purchase location for five years to make informed customer service decisions, enforce our rights and defend against claims; past account and subscription information, which we delete three years after your last account closure to ensure proper and accurate financial planning and reporting; profile data for one year in case of potential litigation, for the filing and enforcement of lawsuits, and for protection against them; data necessary to prevent access by users who have been banned from registering a new account, for the period required to ensure the safety and well-being of our users.
  3. c) Finally, we retain information based on our legitimate interests when there is an unresolved or possible issue, claim or dispute that requires us to retain information (for example, if we receive a valid subpoena or request to retain data (in which case we will need to retain data in accordance with our legal obligations) or if data is required as part of a legal proceeding).
